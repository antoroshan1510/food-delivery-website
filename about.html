    How It All Started

    A journey of passion and flavor

    2018

    The Beginning

    Founded by a group of food enthusiasts who believed that great food should be accessible to everyone. What started as a small kitchen operation quickly grew into something much bigger.

    2020

    Expansion

    We expanded our team and partnered with local farms to source the freshest ingredients. Our commitment to quality and sustainability became the cornerstone of our business.

    2022

    Technology Integration

    Launched our mobile app and website to make ordering easier than ever. Introduced our subscription service for regular customers who want consistent quality meals.

    2024

    National Recognition

    Recognized as one of the top food delivery services in the country. We've served over 1 million happy customers and continue to grow every day.
